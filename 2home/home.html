Statement of Originality

I am Elliott Bunnell. From the age of 10, I have been in love with everything relating to computers, programming, and technology. My grandfather is the person whom I would say fueled this, as he gifted me an Arduino Starter Kit for Christmas. While originally I just messed around with some simple Arduino code, I eventually discovered Python; from there, I have gone on a long path of discovery, where I learn something new pretty much everytime I start writing some code. I first heard about the Catapult program from a friend of mine, and my interest was immediately piqued. An internship that lets me develop a skillset I haven't really found a need to work on yet? And I get paid? Incredible! So I applied, got accepted, and am very excited to see where my future leads. Still unsure of what exactly I want to do, I know technology and programming will play a large role, no matter where I go.
